Bone Broth

Bone Broth Recipe

It is a dish made by simmering the bones and connective tissues of animals. It is delicious and easy to incorporate in various recipes.

Drinking bone broth helps to support joint, skin, and gut health. It also provides vital vitamins, minerals, and amino acids

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon (4 litres) of water
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) apple cider vinegar
  • 2-4 pounds (about 1-2 kg) of animal bones
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a large pot or slow cooker.
  2. Boil the ingredients
  3. Reduce to a simmer and cook for 12-24 hours. The longer it cooks, the better it will taste and more nutritious it will be.
  4. Allow the broth to cool. Strain it into a large container and discard the solids.
